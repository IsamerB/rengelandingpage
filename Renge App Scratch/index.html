Dear Gig-ers,

We’re the ones who follow our passion doing what we love 24/7 in yoga pants and a hoodie and are most likely reading this on our phone. Our worst nightmare is going to work in a suit for 40 hours/week for the next 40 years. Kill me now. And for the love of god, just text me the proposal and don’t dare leave me a voicemail – ain’t nobody got time for that.   

We share everything from office space to cars to even our homes. There are no professional/personal boundaries; we use dating apps and social media for business because why the hell not? Work bleeds into our social lives; as it should, because, when you love what you do - it’s not work.

We don't waste our lives looking forward to the weekend because every day is the weekend. We’re out and about every day and night taking action to turn our fantasies into a reality. We know that it’s not just “who you know” that will get you the next gig but who likes and trusts you. So we’re always trying to meet the right people in hopes that we may one day work together and inspire the world.

But how the hell do we meet the right people? Don’t stress, we got you.
